About

關於

PolyU Campus Radio is operated by the Hong Kong Polytechnic University Student Union Campus Radio Provisional Executive Committee. We aim to provide entertaining and informative programs to members of HKPUSU, as well as to vreate and broadcast promotional soundtracks of other societies within HKPUSU.

理大校園電台由香港理工大學學生會校園電台臨時行政委員會運作，致力通過視聽節目為學生會會員提供娛樂及資訊，同時為學生會內其他屬會提供宣傳聲帶之製作及播放服務。
